Doge Climbs

Doge keeps climbing amidst Elon's Musk's election play. Doge a meme cryptocurrency created for fun later not literally taken over by Elon's Musk's influence surged 30% after news that Donald Trump has won US presidency. Elon Musk who bet big on supporting Trump is now in talks what will be next steps midst this big win. Doge community seemed bullish about this news and dogecoin probably thanks to this news made massive gains in market capitalization.
