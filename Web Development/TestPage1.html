The Imaginary Record Store

Home Page

The Imaginary Record Store holds a range of titles from Bob Dylan to Kate Bush and from Kanye West to Missy Elliot.

Our music collection covers genres including Folk, Jazz, Pop, Rock, Easy Listening, Country music and the Classics.

Pre-owned records

We have a huge range of pre.owned vinyl records from the 1960s to the 1990s including many old favourites and hard-to-find records.

Specialities

We offer a variety of signed releases and limited-edition records.
